# Dr. Toufique Ahmed
**Associate Professor || Materials Informatics || AI in Textiles Researcher || Textile Engineering and Machine Learning**

***My work focuses on leveraging artificial intelligence—specifically Artificial Neural Networks (ANN), XGBoost, and Deep Learning—to optimize manufacturing processes, enhance sustainability, and develop smart functional materials.***

**🚀 Research Focus**

Materials Informatics: Accelerating the discovery of advanced materials using data-driven frameworks.

Predictive Modeling: Applying ML to reduce manufacturing defects (e.g., starting marks in looms) and optimize yarn production.

Sustainability: Developing AI systems to predict and minimize cumulative waste in the weaving and knitting industries.

Smart Textiles: Integrating nanotechnology (green synthesis of AgNPs) with computational analysis for antimicrobial and functional applications.
